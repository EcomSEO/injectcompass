## Strategic notes

**Calculator traffic is 5-10× faster than content traffic.** Calculators ship first, then the supporting posts amplify them via internal linking. Do not flip the order.

**Cross-site canonical plan:** any "how to inject [drug]" or "reconstitution of [compound]" content on PepTips and PepVise canonicals to the InjectCompass version. This is the topical-authority unlock across the three peptide sites (see `docs/injectcompass-research.md` §14).

**Research-context framing is blocking.** Every post referencing a research peptide (BPC-157, TB-500, etc.) carries the standard PepVise-style header: *"For educational and research purposes only. Not intended for human use. Not medical advice."*

**Video is Phase 2.** Launch without video — the text + syringe SVG + printable cheat sheet hits 80% of the value. Video (even cellphone-quality) is a month 3-6 investment.
